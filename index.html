Our Story

Who We Are

Alor Dhara Society (ADHASO) was founded in 2013 in Mirpur, Dhaka with a vision to be a beacon of light — "Alor Dhara" — for people living in poverty and marginalization across Bangladesh.

We believe in community-driven change. Our programs span food security, primary education, skill development, disaster relief, and healthcare outreach. Over 13 years, we've reached thousands of lives.
