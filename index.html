About ADHASO

We Believe Every Life Deserves Light

Alor Dhara Society (ADHASO) was founded in 2013 with a single guiding vision — to bring light into the lives of the underprivileged. Based in Mirpur, Dhaka, we have grown into a nationwide movement with dedicated volunteers, professionals, and passionate changemakers.

We operate across multiple domains including education support, healthcare camps, disaster relief, skill development, and community empowerment — always rooted in compassion and transparency.

Our strength lies in our diversity — from board of trustees to probashi (diaspora) members abroad, everyone contributes to our collective mission of a brighter Bangladesh.
